How to Play

01

Join a Room

Create or join a voice room with your friends. Wait for everyone to connect.

02

Get Your Role

Most players get a secret word. Imposters only get a vague hint about the category.

03

Describe the Word

Take turns speaking aloud to describe the word without giving it away to the imposters.

04

Vote & Win

After everyone speaks, vote on who you think the imposters are. Majority wins!

The Imposter Game is a social deduction game inspired by classic word-guessing games.

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Players are divided into Innocents and Imposters
  2. Innocents receive a secret word
  3. Imposters receive only a hint related to the word
  4. Everyone takes turns describing the word without saying it directly
  5. Imposters must try to blend in despite not knowing the word
  6. After everyone has spoken, players vote on who they think the imposters are
